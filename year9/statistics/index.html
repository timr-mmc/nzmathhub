Strand Overview

#
Strand: Statistics
Year Level: Year 9

About This Strand

The Statistics strand introduces data collection, representation, and analysis. Students learn to interpret statistical information and make informed decisions based on data.

What You'll Learn

  • Collecting and organizing data
  • Statistical graphs and charts
  • Measures of center (mean, median, mode)
  • Measures of spread (range)
  • Interpreting statistical displays
  • Statistical investigations
