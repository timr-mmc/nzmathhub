Strand Overview

#
Strand: Algebra
Year Level: Year 9

About This Strand

The Algebra strand introduces algebraic thinking and builds a foundation for advanced mathematics. Students learn about patterns, expressions, equations, and how to use symbols to represent mathematical relationships.

What You'll Learn

  • Algebraic expressions and notation
  • Simplifying expressions
  • Solving linear equations
  • Patterns and sequences
  • Substitution into formulas
  • Graphing linear relationships
