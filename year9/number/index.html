Strand Overview

#
Strand: Number
Year Level: Year 9

About This Strand

The Number strand focuses on developing number sense and computational fluency. Students explore integers, fractions, decimals, percentages, and various number operations.

What You'll Learn

  • Integers and integer operations
  • Fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • Order of operations
  • Powers and square roots
  • Ratio and proportion
  • Number properties and patterns
