Strand Overview

Strand: Measurement
Year Level: Year 9

About This Strand

The Measurement strand develops skills in measuring and calculating length, area, volume, and other quantities. Students learn to use appropriate units and formulas for practical measurement problems.

What You'll Learn

  • Metric units and conversions
  • Perimeter and area of 2D shapes
  • Surface area and volume of 3D objects
  • Time, distance, and speed
  • Scale and measurement precision
  • Pythagoras' theorem
