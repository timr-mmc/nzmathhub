Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91946 - Interpret Statistical Info
Credits: 5
Level: NCEA Level 1

Achievement Standard

This standard involves interpreting statistical information and datasets. Students will analyze data displays, identify trends and patterns, and make informed conclusions based on statistical evidence.

What You'll Learn

  • Reading and interpreting graphs and charts
  • Analyzing data distributions
  • Identifying trends and patterns in data
  • Making comparisons between datasets
  • Drawing evidence-based conclusions
