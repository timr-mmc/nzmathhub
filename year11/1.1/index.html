Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91944 - Statistical Enquiry
Credits: 5
Level: NCEA Level 1

Achievement Standard

This standard involves carrying out a statistical enquiry process. Students will plan and conduct investigations, gather and process data, and use statistical methods to explore a question of interest.

What You'll Learn

  • Planning a statistical investigation
  • Collecting and organizing data
  • Using appropriate statistical methods
  • Communicating findings with evidence
  • Drawing conclusions from data analysis
