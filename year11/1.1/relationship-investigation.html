What is a Relationship Investigation?

#
Focus: A relationship investigation explores how two variables are connected and how changes in one variable may relate to changes in another.

Key Features

  • Involves two or more variables
  • Explores associations and patterns
  • Uses scatter plots and correlation analysis
  • Examines the strength and direction of relationships
  • Considers confounding variables

Example Questions

  • What is the relationship between study time and test scores for Year 11 students?
  • What is the relationship between temperature and ice cream sales at the school canteen?
  • What is the relationship between height and shoe size for students at our school?
  • What is the relationship between hours of exercise per week and resting heart rate for teenagers?
