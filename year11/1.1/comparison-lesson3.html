Lesson Introduction

#
Learning Objectives: In this lesson, you will learn how to analyze distributions, compare groups using statistical measures, and draw justified conclusions.

What You'll Learn

  • Comparing measures of center (mean, median)
  • Comparing measures of spread (range, IQR)
  • Interpreting shape and distribution
  • Identifying outliers and unusual features
  • Drawing evidence-based conclusions
  • Considering limitations and improvements
