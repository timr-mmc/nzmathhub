Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91947 - Mathematical Reasoning
Credits: 5
Level: NCEA Level 1

Achievement Standard

This standard involves applying mathematical reasoning in solving problems. Students will develop logical arguments, justify their mathematical thinking, and prove mathematical statements using appropriate reasoning.

What You'll Learn

  • Constructing mathematical arguments
  • Using logical reasoning to solve problems
  • Justifying mathematical conclusions
  • Proving mathematical relationships
  • Evaluating mathematical statements
