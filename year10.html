Mathematics Strands for Year 10

Choose a mathematics strand to access learning resources and activities.

Number

Advance your understanding of rational numbers, irrational numbers, indices, and scientific notation.

Algebra

Solve linear equations, work with formulas, and explore quadratic patterns. Strengthen algebraic manipulation skills.

Geometry

Study coordinate geometry, similarity, congruence, and circle geometry. Develop geometric proof skills.

Measurement

Explore Pythagoras' theorem, trigonometry basics, and complex measurement problems involving 2D and 3D shapes.

Statistics

Analyze bivariate data, create scatter plots, and understand measures of center and spread. Conduct statistical investigations.

Probability

Calculate theoretical and experimental probabilities. Explore compound events and probability models.
