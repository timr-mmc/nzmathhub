Mathematics Strands for Year 9

#

Choose a mathematics strand to access learning resources and activities.

Number

Explore integers, fractions, decimals, percentages, and number operations. Develop number sense and computational fluency.

Algebra

Learn about patterns, expressions, equations, and algebraic thinking. Build a foundation for advanced mathematics.

Geometry

Study shapes, angles, transformations, and spatial reasoning. Understand geometric properties and relationships.

Measurement

Work with length, area, volume, time, and units of measurement. Apply measurement concepts to real-world situations.

Statistics

Collect, organize, display, and interpret data. Learn about statistical investigations and data analysis.

Probability

Understand chance and uncertainty. Calculate probabilities and explore random events and outcomes.
