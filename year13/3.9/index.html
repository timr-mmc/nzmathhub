Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91581 - Bivariate
Credits: 4
Level: NCEA Level 3

Achievement Standard

This standard involves investigating bivariate measurement data. Students will analyze relationships between two numerical variables.

What You'll Learn

  • Scatter plots and correlation
  • Linear regression and least squares
  • Residual analysis
  • Non-linear models and transformations
  • Making predictions and assessing model fit
2

Course Lessons

#

This bivariate data course is divided into 8 comprehensive lessons covering all aspects of bivariate analysis:

Lesson 1

Introduction to Bivariate Data

Understanding two-variable relationships, types of relationships, and real-world examples.

 Lesson 2

Scatter Plots

Creating, describing, and interpreting scatter plots using the DFSU framework.

 Lesson 3

Correlation Coefficient

Understanding, calculating, and interpreting the correlation coefficient (r).

 Lesson 4

Line of Best Fit

Linear regression, least squares method, and the coefficient of determination (R²).

 Lesson 5

Making Predictions

Using regression equations, interpolation, and the dangers of extrapolation.

 Lesson 6

Outliers and Unusual Features

Identifying outliers, influential points, clusters, gaps, and other unusual features.

 Interactive

Interactive Scatter Plot Tool

Hands-on tool to explore correlations, clustering, outliers, and predictions.

 Practice

Practice Problems

Test your understanding with basic, intermediate, and advanced practice problems.
