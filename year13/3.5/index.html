Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91577 - Complex
Credits: 5
Level: NCEA Level 3

Achievement Standard

This standard involves applying complex number methods in solving problems. Students will work with complex numbers in various forms and contexts.

What You'll Learn

  • Operations with complex numbers
  • Cartesian and polar forms
  • De Moivre's theorem
  • Solving equations with complex numbers
  • Geometric representation of complex numbers
