Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91586 - Distributions
Credits: 4
Level: NCEA Level 3

Achievement Standard

This standard involves applying probability distributions in solving problems. Students will work with discrete and continuous distributions.

What You'll Learn

  • Binomial distribution
  • Normal distribution
  • Poisson distribution
  • Expected values and variance
  • Applying distributions to real-world problems
