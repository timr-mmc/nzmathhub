Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91573 - Conics
Credits: 3
Level: NCEA Level 3

Achievement Standard

This standard involves applying the geometry of conic sections in solving problems. Students will work with parabolas, circles, ellipses, and hyperbolas.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding conic sections and their properties
  • Working with equations of circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas
  • Transformations of conic sections
  • Solving problems involving conic sections
  • Applying geometric properties in problem-solving
