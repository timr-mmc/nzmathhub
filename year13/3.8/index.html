Standard Overview

#
Standard: AS91580 - Time Series
Credits: 4
Level: NCEA Level 3

Achievement Standard

This standard involves investigating time series data. Students will analyze data that changes over time and identify patterns and trends.

What You'll Learn

  • Components of time series (trend, seasonal, irregular)
  • Smoothing techniques and moving averages
  • Identifying and analyzing trends
  • Making predictions from time series data
  • Interpreting time series graphs and patterns
