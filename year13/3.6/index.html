3.6 Differentiation

Master the techniques of differentiation and apply them to solve real-world problems. This comprehensive section covers everything from basic rules to advanced applications including optimisation and related rates of change.

📚 10 Interactive Lessons | ⏱️ Progressive Difficulty | 🎯 Exam-Focused Practice

Learning Pathway

Follow the lessons in order for the best learning experience. Each lesson builds on previous concepts.

Lesson 1

Basic Differentiation: The Power Rule

Start with the fundamental rule of differentiation. Learn to differentiate polynomials with confidence using minimal change problems.

Topics:
  • Power rule for positive powers
  • Differentiating constants
  • Negative and fractional powers
Lesson 2

Product & Quotient Rules

Learn when and how to apply the product and quotient rules for more complex functions.

Topics:
  • Product rule: (uv)' = u'v + uv'
  • Quotient rule: (u/v)' = (u'v - uv')/v²
  • When to use each rule
Lesson 3

The Chain Rule

Master composite functions with the chain rule. Essential for more advanced calculus.

Topics:
  • Chain rule: dy/dx = dy/du × du/dx
  • Identifying composite functions
  • Multiple compositions
Lesson 4

Mixed Techniques Practice

Combine all differentiation rules. Learn to identify which technique to use for any function.

Topics:
  • Deciding which rule to apply
  • Combining multiple rules
  • Simplifying derivatives
Lesson 5

Tangents & Normals

Apply derivatives to find equations of tangent lines and normal lines to curves.

Topics:
  • Finding gradients at specific points
  • Equation of tangent: y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)
  • Perpendicular gradients for normals
Lesson 6

Stationary Points

Find and classify maximum and minimum points using first and second derivatives.

Topics:
  • Finding stationary points (f'(x) = 0)
  • First derivative test
  • Second derivative test
Lesson 7

Points of Inflection

Understand concavity and find points where the curve changes from concave up to concave down.

Topics:
  • Second derivative and concavity
  • Finding inflection points (f''(x) = 0)
  • Interpreting curve behavior
Lesson 8

Related Rates of Change

Solve problems where multiple quantities change with respect to time.

Topics:
  • Implicit differentiation with time
  • Connected rates: dV/dt, dh/dt
  • Real-world applications
Lesson 9

Optimisation Problems

Use calculus to find maximum and minimum values in real-world contexts.

Topics:
  • Setting up optimisation problems
  • Constraint equations
  • Practical applications: areas, volumes, costs
Lesson 10

Advanced Applications

Tackle complex problems combining multiple techniques. Prepare for NCEA assessments.

Topics:
  • Complex optimisation scenarios
  • Advanced related rates
  • Exam-style questions

Study Tips for Success

📝 Practice Regularly

Master each technique with minimal change problems before moving to the next lesson.

🔄 Review Often

Revisit earlier lessons using spaced repetition for better long-term retention.

✍️ Show Your Working

Always write out each step clearly. This helps identify errors and builds good exam habits.
